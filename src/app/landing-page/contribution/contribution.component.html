Contribution

The application is designed to adapt to diverse oxalate-related needs, providing features such as meal visibility with detailed oxalate content to ensure inclusivity for all users. By offering tools that allow users to track and manage oxalate intake effectively, the app serves as a valuable resource for those with specific dietary requirements.

Contributors can expand on this foundation by developing innovative features that enhance functionality, improve user experience, and address a broader range of oxalate management challenges, ultimately benefiting a wider audience.

By contributing, you not only help maintain and improve the application but also play a vital role in creating a reliable, community-driven resource for oxalate tracking and dietary management. Together, we can ensure the app evolves to meet the changing needs of its users while fostering a collaborative and inclusive environment for innovation.

How

Contributing to the application is simple and accessible. Users can support the maintenance and development of the app by opting into a monthly contribution of $2. This small amount helps cover ongoing costs and ensures the app continues to evolve and meet the needs of the community.

Additionally, users who wish to provide further support are welcome to make additional one-time donations. These contributions enable the development of new features, improved functionality, and enhanced user experiences, making the app even more valuable for everyone. Your support, no matter the amount, helps build a stronger, more sustainable platform for oxalate tracking and management.

Thank you for helping to maintain and grow this community-driven resource!
